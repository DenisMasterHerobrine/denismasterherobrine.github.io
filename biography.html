Gaming

Basically my journey started from 2011 when I got my first laptop shared with my family members. I have been playing old arcade games, just because they were friendly and funny.

2 months (or so?) later I had discovered Youtube and first betas of Minecraft. I was so excited about it, so I started to watch videos about the game. The first one was about redstone. Up to 2013 I was playing the beta versions of Minecraft, then I got able to play Modded Minecraft starting from beta 1.9.1 with IndustrialCraft 2, BuildCraft and TooManyItems as far as I know.

Then I started playing more modern versions such as 1.2.5, 1.5.2. Mostly I played 1.5.2 up to time when I was able to buy the game, because the launcher was changed and basically reworked from scratch. I played a lot of modpacks, but the most favourite one was Tekkit Classic. I was playing it for a long time, but then I got bored and started to play other modpacks. Up to 2024 I have completed mostly 2000 of modpacks and still counting.

As of 2020 I started playing other games, because I was feeling that I'm getting tired from Minecraft. Seriously, I was playing it literally more than 40k hours (if MultiMC stats not wrong, lol) and I needed a break from the game.

I was streaming on Twitch for a while. I was streaming mostly Minecraft, but sometimes I was streaming other games such as DOOM, Halo, etc, because my viewers requested them in Discord. So, after 2020 I started discovering different games and now I'm feeling much better about gaming, because I used to play only Minecraft and nothing else. I wanted to play only MC before.

When 2022 started, I started to get much better at programming and started progressively modding the games and started coding cool stuff and doing variety stuff.

I have also discovered Destiny 2, thanks to my friends. I had played it 2000 hours and counting, but I felt that was a bit boring as of the start of 2024, so I only complete challenges or raids/dungeons with my friends.

I have also played DOOM a lot and this game motivated me to do something in my life. I was playing it for a long time and I have completed it on Nightmare difficulty. It was en exciting journey and memorizable one, so I started to show interest in space-related projects and in science fiction. It motivated me to learn more about space and space exploration. We will talk about it later.
